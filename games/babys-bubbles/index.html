About the game

Popping bubbles has never been more fun!

Your babies and toddlers will love this eye-catching game full of animals, instruments, vehicles and much more.

Baby's bubbles is incredibly easy to play. Just tap on the screen and surprise yourself. What's that sound? What's that thing? An elephant or course! Oh, what's that! Toot, Toot! It's a car, just like dad's.

Bewilder your babies or toddlers with a variety of images and sounds which will improve your kids memory, timing, focusing and knowledge.

This game is exceptionally handy for parents who want to spend time with their kids and teach them about a variety of things.

This game may be too advanced for infants under 6 months old.
